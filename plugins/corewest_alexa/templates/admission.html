Admission Process

A simple, transparent, four-step journey to joining Core West College

1
📋

Inquiry

Contact our admissions team by phone, email, or by visiting the school to learn about our programmes and available spaces.

2
📝

Application

Complete and submit the application form along with the required supporting documents for your child's desired division.

3
🧠

Assessment

Your child will attend a friendly, low-pressure assessment session to help us place them in the most appropriate year group and learning pathway.

4
✅

Enrollment

Upon successful placement, complete the enrollment paperwork and fees to confirm your child's place for the upcoming academic year.
