📅 March 2026 — All Divisions

DEAR Week Celebration

Drop Everything and Read — a school-wide literacy celebration encouraging students of all ages to discover the joy of reading. Special author visits, reading challenges, and a school-wide read-aloud event make this a highlight of the academic calendar.

Literacy
🎉

📅 February 2026 — Early Years & KG

100 Days of Learning

Early Years and KG students celebrated their 100th day of school with themed activities, counting games, creative projects, and costumes — a beloved tradition that marks a major milestone in the school year.

Milestone
🔬

📅 April 2026 — Grades 5–11

Science Fair 2026

Students from Grades 5–11 showcased their innovative science experiments and research projects before a panel of judges. Projects ranged from environmental sustainability to robotics, demonstrating the breadth of scientific thinking at Core West.

STEM
🌙

📅 October 2025 — All Divisions

National Day Celebration

A joyful school-wide celebration of Egyptian national pride, featuring traditional costumes, cultural performances, food stalls, and a special assembly honouring Egypt's rich heritage and history.

Culture
🌍

📅 November 2025 — All Divisions

International Day

Students and families from over 20 nationalities came together to celebrate the diversity of our school community. Each class represented a different country with displays, performances, and traditional foods shared with the whole school family.

Community
🎓

📅 June 2026 — All Divisions

End of Year Ceremony

The annual End of Year Ceremony brings together students, parents, and staff to celebrate academic achievements, present awards, honour graduating students, and reflect on a year full of growth, learning, and memorable moments.
