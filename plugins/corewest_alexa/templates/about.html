Who We Are

Core West College is a premier international institution located in the 1st Neighborhood, District 13, El Sheikh Zayed, Egypt. We proudly serve students across four distinct academic divisions — International Early Years, American, British, and National — offering a rich, multilingual educational experience that celebrates both global standards and Egyptian heritage.

Founded on the vision of making world-class education accessible in Egypt, Core West College has grown into a thriving community of learners, educators, and families united by a shared passion for excellence. Our school environment is inclusive, nurturing, and intellectually challenging, preparing students for success in universities and careers across the globe.

Our Founder & Leadership

MG

Mr. Mahmoud Gohar

Chairman & CEO — BBA, MBA

Mr. Mahmoud Gohar is the visionary Chairman and CEO of Core West College. With a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) and a Master of Business Administration (MBA), he brings decades of strategic and operational expertise to the educational sector.

Coming from a family with a rich tradition of investing in education since 1988, Mr. Gohar established Core West College with a clear mission: to build an institution that rivals the best international schools in the region while remaining deeply rooted in Egyptian values and culture. Under his leadership, the college has grown into a multi-division institution serving hundreds of students from across Sheikh Zayed City and the greater Cairo area.

Mr. Gohar believes that quality education is the most powerful investment a society can make — and he remains personally committed to ensuring that every student at Core West College receives the tools, guidance, and inspiration they need to succeed.

SM

Mrs. Shereen Moussad

Head of Schools

Mrs. Shereen Moussad serves as the Head of Schools at Core West College, bringing over 20 years of distinguished experience in both the American and British educational systems. Her deep understanding of international pedagogical frameworks, curriculum design, and school leadership has been transformative for the institution.

Mrs. Moussad is a passionate advocate for student-centred learning, continuous professional development for teachers, and creating school cultures where every learner feels valued and capable. Her expertise spans early childhood education through secondary levels, and she is committed to ensuring that each of Core West's four divisions operates to the very highest academic and pastoral standards.

Under her academic leadership, Core West College has implemented world-class teaching frameworks, earned recognition from international educational organisations, and built a reputation for outstanding student outcomes across all divisions.
