Our Teaching Approach

Interactive Learning

Recognised by Twinkl for our commitment to interactive, resource-rich learning environments. Our classrooms are dynamic spaces where students actively engage with content through hands-on activities, collaborative projects, and inquiry-led investigations.

Technology Integration

Seamless integration of digital tools — including AI-assisted learning, smart boards, educational platforms, and voice-activated classroom assistants — ensures students develop the digital literacy skills essential for the modern world.

Student-Centred Pedagogy

Every lesson is designed with the individual learner in mind. Teachers differentiate instruction to meet diverse learning needs, ensuring all students are appropriately challenged, supported, and celebrated.

Teaching Quality Frameworks

Our teachers are evaluated and developed using internationally recognised professional frameworks

🇬🇧

Ofsted Framework

British Division

The Ofsted Inspection Framework guides the quality assurance and professional development of teachers in our British Division. We evaluate lesson quality against Ofsted criteria for Outstanding teaching, covering:

  • ✅ Quality of education and curriculum intent
  • ✅ Behaviour and attitudes of learners
  • ✅ Personal development beyond the academic
  • ✅ Leadership and management effectiveness
  • ✅ Safeguarding and student wellbeing
🇺🇸

Danielson Framework

American Division

The Danielson Framework for Teaching provides the professional standards for our American Division educators. It evaluates teaching effectiveness across four domains:

  • ✅ Domain 1: Planning and Preparation
  • ✅ Domain 2: The Classroom Environment
  • ✅ Domain 3: Instruction and Engagement
  • ✅ Domain 4: Professional Responsibilities
  • ✅ Continuous feedback and coaching cycles
