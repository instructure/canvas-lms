Pre-K – Year 2

International Early Years

Our International Early Years division provides a warm, nurturing environment where the youngest learners develop the foundational skills, confidence, and curiosity they need for a lifetime of learning. Grounded in the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) framework, our approach is play-based, child-centred, and developmentally appropriate.

  • ✅ EYFS-aligned curriculum
  • ✅ Play-based and inquiry learning
  • ✅ Multilingual immersion (English, Arabic)
  • ✅ Social-emotional development focus
  • ✅ Low student-teacher ratios
  • ✅ Dedicated outdoor learning spaces

Grade 3 – Grade 11

American Division

The American Division delivers a rigorous, internationally recognised curriculum aligned with Common Core State Standards. Our programme develops critical thinkers, creative problem-solvers, and confident communicators prepared for university study in the United States and beyond.

With an emphasis on project-based learning, STEM education, and college-readiness, students in the American Division acquire the academic skills and intellectual habits needed to excel in competitive higher education environments.

  • ✅ Common Core Standards alignment
  • ✅ STEM and project-based learning
  • ✅ College-readiness preparation
  • ✅ Advanced English Language Arts
  • ✅ Critical thinking and debate

Year 3 – Year 11

British Division

Following the British National Curriculum with a pathway towards IGCSE qualifications, our British Division is renowned for its academic rigour, structured progression, and development of independent learners. Students benefit from a broad and balanced curriculum covering sciences, humanities, arts, and languages.

Our British Division teachers are experienced in UK pedagogical frameworks and employ the Ofsted-recognised practices that characterise outstanding schools worldwide.

  • ✅ British National Curriculum
  • ✅ IGCSE examination preparation
  • ✅ Ofsted-aligned teaching standards
  • ✅ Strong humanities & arts programmes
  • ✅ Independent learning development

National Division

Our National Division delivers the Egyptian Ministry of Education curriculum with added enrichment programmes that celebrate Arabic language excellence, Islamic studies, and national cultural heritage. We bridge tradition and modernity, giving students a strong sense of identity while preparing them for Egypt's national examinations.

  • ✅ Egyptian Ministry of Education curriculum
  • ✅ Arabic language excellence programme
  • ✅ National examination preparation
  • ✅ Cultural heritage and values
  • ✅ Enrichment in sciences and technology
