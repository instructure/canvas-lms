A World-Class Learning Environment

Core West College's campus in the 1st Neighborhood, District 13, El Sheikh Zayed is designed to support every dimension of student life — academic, creative, physical, and social. Our modern facilities provide the infrastructure for outstanding teaching and learning across all four academic divisions.

Our Facilities

🏫

Modern Classrooms

Bright, spacious, technology-equipped classrooms with interactive smart boards, air conditioning, and ergonomic furniture designed to support diverse teaching styles and collaborative learning.

  • ✅ Smart boards in every room
  • ✅ Flexible seating arrangements
  • ✅ Dedicated division-specific wings
🔬

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped science labs for Biology, Chemistry, and Physics, enabling hands-on experimentation and inquiry-based learning from primary through secondary levels.

  • ✅ Separate Biology, Chemistry & Physics labs
  • ✅ Safety-compliant equipment
  • ✅ STEM project workspaces
💻

Computer Labs

State-of-the-art computer laboratories supporting coding, digital literacy, graphic design, and research skills development across all year groups.

  • ✅ High-speed internet access
  • ✅ Coding and robotics programmes
  • ✅ Digital creativity tools
📚

Library & Resource Centre

A well-stocked, bilingual library offering thousands of titles in English and Arabic, digital databases, reading corners, and a dedicated research area for secondary students.

  • ✅ Bilingual English & Arabic collection
  • ✅ Digital research databases
  • ✅ Quiet study and group reading zones
⚽

Sports Facilities

Comprehensive sports areas supporting physical education, inter-school competitions, and healthy lifestyle development for students across all ages and abilities.

  • ✅ Multi-purpose sports court
  • ✅ Football and outdoor play areas
  • ✅ PE curriculum for all year groups
🎨

Creative Arts Studios

Dedicated art studios and music spaces nurturing creativity, self-expression, and appreciation of the arts — an integral part of a well-rounded Core West education.

  • ✅ Visual arts and ceramics studio
  • ✅ Music and performance space
  • ✅ Drama and presentation area
